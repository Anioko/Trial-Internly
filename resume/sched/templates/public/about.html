What Internly is

Internly is an online recruitment portal, which is match-making companies, and people in need of work. Our vision is to become #1 in Europe when it comes recruiting interns, entry- level workers and experienced professionals. We make it easier for companies to find applicants for their needs.

Why? It takes average of 6 seconds for recruiter to read a resumé, or CV. When there are hundreds or even thousands of applicants, it will take huge amount of time to go through them. We shorten that time, and only show the best fitting applicants for recruiters.

How? Applicant creates a CV, which only has the most necessary information for recruiters. Applicants use one-click ”Apply Now” system, to numerous open positions. The recruiter sees only those, who are fitting for the position, and can download all CVs that seems interesting for their needs.
